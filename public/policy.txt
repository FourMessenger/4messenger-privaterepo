Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how 4 Messenger ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") collects, uses, discloses, and otherwise processes personal information in connection with our messaging platform and related services (collectively, the "Service").

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information in the following ways:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- Account Registration: When you create an account, we collect information such as your username, email address, and password.
- Communication Data: Messages, files, media, and other content you send through our Service.
- Profile Information: Any additional information you voluntarily provide, such as profile pictures or status messages.
- Support Communications: Any information you provide when contacting our support team.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- Technical Data: Information about your device, browser type, operating system, and IP address.
- Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Service, including access times and pages viewed.
- Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use collected information for the following purposes:

- Providing and improving the Service
- Communicating with you about account, security, or support matters
- Personalizing your experience
- Analyzing usage patterns to enhance functionality
- Complying with legal obligations
- Preventing fraud and ensuring security

4. ENCRYPTION AND SECURITY

4.1 End-to-End Encryption
All messages and communications within 4 Messenger are protected by end-to-end encryption. This means only you and the intended recipient can read your messages. We do not have access to the content of your encrypted communications.

4.2 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:
- Encrypted data transmission using TLS/SSL protocols
- Secure storage of authentication credentials
- Regular security audits and updates

5. DATA SHARING AND THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

- With service providers who assist in operating our Service (subject to data protection agreements)
- When required by law or legal process
- To prevent fraud or protect security
- As necessary to enforce our Terms of Service

6. DATA RETENTION

- Message Data: Encrypted messages are stored for the duration of your service usage. You may delete messages at any time.
- Account Data: Account information is retained for the duration of your account. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days, except where required by law.
- Backup Data: Encrypted backups may be retained for disaster recovery purposes.

7. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

- Access: Request access to your personal information
- Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
- Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
- Opt-out: Control communication preferences

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. DATA PRIVACY BY DESIGN

Our Service is built with privacy-first principles:
- Minimal Data Collection: We collect only essential data
- Local Processing: Much of your data remains on your device
- Transparency: You have visibility into what data is collected
- User Control: You maintain control over sharing settings
